Support Beyond Healthcare
At Xpertise Medical, our non-medical services provide vital support that complements traditional healthcare, addressing needs that encompass personal care, companionship, and assistance with daily living activities. Our services are designed to help individuals live safely and comfortably at home and give families confidence that their loved ones are receiving dependable care.
Respite Care
Offers a crucial break for caregivers, ensuring their loved ones receive professional care and attention during their absence, promoting well-being for both.
Companionship
Enhances quality of life through social interaction and safety supervision, ensuring individuals feel connected, supported, and secure in their environment.
Medication Reminders
Provides essential support in medication management, ensuring timely and accurate intake to maintain health and prevent complications.
Diet and Meal Preparation
Focuses on nutritional health by preparing balanced meals tailored to individual dietary needs, promoting overall well-being through proper nutrition.
Monitoring Vital Signs
Regular checks of vital signs like blood pressure, glucose, and oxygen levels to monitor health status and prevent potential health issues.
Personal Care and Hygiene
Ensures dignity and cleanliness through personal grooming and hygiene assistance, while also observing for signs of health changes or conditions.
Home Making
Maintains a clean and orderly living space with services like light housekeeping and laundry, contributing to a comfortable and healthy home environment.
Alzheimer’s and Dementia Care
Provides specialized care to maintain safety, security, and activity levels, ensuring individuals with cognitive impairments live their best possible life.
Hospice Support
Providing caregiving support for individuals receiving hospice care, working alongside hospice providers to assist with daily needs and family support.
